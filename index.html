About PocketResume

Keep your resume in your pocket. PocketResume is a premium Progressive Web App (PWA) that lets you modify your professional profile anytime, anywhere, and share it instantly. We've eliminated the complexity of traditional resume apps and removed all privacy concerns by keeping your data entirely on your device.

Advanced AI Features

PocketResume leverages smart AI algorithms to streamline the resume-building process:

  • AI Prefilling: Instantly populate your entire profile by uploading any existing resume or pasting raw LinkedIn data. Our smart parser extracts experience, skills, and contact info with 99% accuracy.
  • AI Chat Assistant: Use our integrated AI chat to rewrite job descriptions, optimize for specific ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems), and get real-time feedback on your professional summary.

Mobile Optimization & Scaling

PocketResume is engineered for a seamless mobile experience:

  • Auto-Scaling UI: Our advanced layout engine automatically scales the resume to fit any screen size perfectly, ensuring readability without manual scrolling.
  • Smart Zoom Stability: We've implemented technical safeguards to stabilize the view during pinch-zoom, preventing layout shifts and maintaining a crisp, centered resume.
  • Worker-Thread Parsing: PDF analysis runs on a background worker thread, keeping the user interface smooth and responsive even during heavy data extraction.

Premium Design & UX

Unlike generic builders, PocketResume offers a cinematic and professional interface:

  • Liquid Border UI: Floating toolbars with animated multi-color "liquid flow" border effects.
  • Information Density: Optimized layouts that prioritize content over decorative icons, ensuring your resume remains professional, dense, and clear.
  • Offline Privacy: As a PWA, your data never leaves your device, providing maximum security and lightning-fast performance without an internet connection.

Privacy & Performance

As a PWA, PocketResume works entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, providing maximum privacy and lightning-fast performance even when you are offline.
